Axie Infinity Card Beginner's Guide

The Axie Infinity contains over 132 cards organized into six categories: Aqua, Beast, Plant, Bird, Bug, and Reptile. It's then divided into components (tail, back, mouth, horn) as well as range and costs.

If we want to have a high win rate in Arena Mode, we must be familiar with these cards, their effects, and combos. The majority of them deal a lot of damage, and some of them are good when coupled with other cards for defending, setting up, and providing buffs and debuffs.
