Axie Infinity Developer Acknowledges Sharp Usage Drop

Axie Infinity developer, Sky Mavis, tweeted some important information last night, responding to Wagner James Au tweet citing Arcane Research, which originally claimed that the "play to earn" game had slipped beneath 1000 weekly active users. On the contrary, COO Aleksander Leonard Larsen said that the accurate number is currently 588,000 DAU / 1.5 million DAU.

Notably, this is still a major drop in usage for Axie Infinity, based on the Google Chart that Larsen shared to bolster this claim: According to the data, Axie Infinity's daily active users have shrunk from 1,331,453 on April 11 down to 588,087 on June 6.

"We are in a major transition period in our game, narrative and economy, as well as it being a macro bear market", Axie Infinity product lead Philip La replied in the Twitter thread, acknowledging the drop."The user drop is a function of all of these, each of which have their own complex nuances."


Axie Infinity Esports Tournament Rocks the Philippines

Sixteen players participated in the two-day Axie Infinity Origins Championship that featured a 400 $AXS prize pool.


Popular Ronin Network, PlayandEarn blockchain game, Axie Infinity, recently held one of its biggest Esports tournament in the Philippines.

Half of the player pool got invited to the two-day event from June 11-12, while the other half had to get past a qualification round held the month before. The Axie Infinity Origins Championship, which had a 400 $AXS prize pool, got Axie's Philippine Lead, Nix Eniego, really excited for the game's future and its impact on the community.

"I see the first-ever Axie Origin PH Championship as a success," Nix told BSC News."Seeing the entire team and top competitors come together and pour everything they have was inspiring. Our eSports team [and] our production partners did a phenomenal job in giving the #AxiePH community a taste of competitive Axie Origin eSports with some entertainment sprinkled on top."

Both finalists needed to qualify to join the tournament. But in the end, ADRD, who had to climb the lower brackets to reach the finals, bested undefeated P4TRICK in 4 matches. The Polish player saw himself in 0-1 deficit to start the finals but rallied behind his unique team compositions and sealed the three-game win streak with his blood moon poison strategy to take home the trophy.


What Lunacia Land Gameplay Is Bringing With It?

According to the roadmap, Axie Infinity Land Gameplay was anticipated to be released in 2022.

In Axie Infinity Land Gameplay, players will explore the open world to discover resource nodes or "Chimera".

Chimeras are enemies in game which the Axies will battle against. Players will chose the team of their creatures to fight against them. Fols will have teleportation option for their Axies.

Once teleported, Axies cannot be sent back home via teleportation, and they have to travel home back, which might take a few days. Mean players can teleport the creatures in the battle instantly, but cannot do that immediately if they desire to send Axies back to base.

Additionally, the update will include off-chain pawn Axies to organize various tasks on the Land. In brief, Axie Infinity Land Gameplay will allow folks to take ownership of a peice of land and use it to develop resources which can be upgraded later in game.

This new Axie Infinity Land Gameplay is similar to the Clash of Clans, a battle strategy game. Players in CoC gather resources, make raids, and defend their bases from other players.
